A IAT 336 ProjectIntroduction:

This project is a VR design project that I worked with three other group members on Fall, 2022. The goal of this project is to produce a cool, unique and comfortable device.

The whole process took about four weeks and five major steps. The installation is based on a box. At the top center of the box, there is a ball back and forth. Beside the box, there is a hand holding on a box. The hand is keep managing to tipping the ball. Yet the chance the hand can reach the ball is fully random.

The installation build up with several different forms of transmission. There are different combinations of cranks works together to drive the finger move. There are also gears and transmission shaft to deliver the power to the hands.

Step 1: Ideation and Sketching

Ideation

To acieve that purpose, we started tons of research to come up with some cool mechanism.

Sketching

The next step was to sketch components with a rough density to see whether our design works. To do so, we drew the key component and its size to calculate whether our size was in a correct range.

Build Digital Model In Software

For the last step of this section, we have build the sketch in to digital model based on the size we calculate, and adjuest the incorrect size. This step is critical for us, since it can simulate the real model and tell us the direction we need to improve and the mistake we need to fixed before we actally laser cut our components.

Step 2: Components Modeling

Rough Model

After we finished the digital design, we started build our physical rough model the purpose of this was to build a rough model with a card paper to see is there is any design flaws that we havn't covered in the digital design part.

Functioning Check

Another Important thing we need to do in this step is to check whether each functions we designed works perfectly as we expected. Especiatelly the mechanism structure. After we checked every step, we started to simplify the design. The goal of this process was to create a device that can make same or better function with more simple mechnism.

Step 3: Assembling

Planning laser cut

Since then, we started planning laser cut our model and assembled it into the fully function model. During this process, we have have designed several sets of spare parts in case we have mistakenly use or broke our laser cut pieces.

Embroidery Choosing

In order to enhance the unique of our project, we decide to use embroidery to decorate our device and help reduce its weight.

Step 4: Final Product

Assembling

After we get the laser cut piece, we started to assembled those pieces into our model. during this process, we have still faced some challenge like the glue works on card paper, but not wood. It tooks us few more time on solving those unexpected problems. But lucklly, our prepareing work was well-considered and that save us huge amount of time.

Final Model

Finally, we got our fully-functional model.
